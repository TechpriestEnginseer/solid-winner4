Location: In orbit around the Aztlan Relay in the Aztlan System.
Date: 194.01.01

The Aztlan Relay has been giving off strange readings and we have heard nothing from our packet ships. An envoy from House Celeste of Eventide has offered to investigate the strange readings.

Our resources are bled dry and we're unable to spare anymore combat ships aside from an Enforcer, but the envoy merely requested an additional pair of Condor-class light carriers.

Upon the envoy's return, she brought reports corresponding with other patrol reports that a large Tri-Tachyon force was en route to Aztlan, very likely Chicomoztoc...

Completing this mission unlocks an Iron Shell Super Ship start in Nexerelin.
